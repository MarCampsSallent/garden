Berardo: My Little Garden

Hello gardener!

Are you passionate about growing your own fruits and vegetables? Do you adore the outdoors and gardening? Say no more!

You're in the right place...

We are a Barcelona-based, family-run enterprise that enables people to grow, garden and care for their nutrition.

At Berardo, you will have the option of having your own garden outside of the busy metropolian area, where you can grow your own fruits, vegetables or flowers!

Don't wait anymore and contact us today!

How does it work?

  1. You choose one of the packages we offer, based on how long you'd like to have a garden at Berardo
  2. You get in touch with us - see our contact info below or Visit us
  3. We will follow up via email or phone and answer any questions you might have
  4. One of our Berardo gardens is all YOURS - ready to grow and nurture veggies, fruits and flowers!
