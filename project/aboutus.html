Our Mission

Berardo: My Little Garden is a small urban farming business based just outside Barcelona.

As we work, we are developing, as well as analyzing, the potential for economic viability in the field of urban farming.

We seek to serve a diverse population, and to build a community dialogue about food systems, by involving and educating members in our processes and problem solving.

Our Values

In our garden, and in our lives, we value:

  • creativity
  • collective decision-making
  • transparency
  • sensitivity to people and place
  • bold ideas
  • humility

Our Story

2004-2007

Hermann Camps grew up cultivating the land around him and wanted to share that experience to those who didn't have acess as a result of living in a large metropolis.

Before any planning, designing or construction occurs, Hermann conducts a community readiness assessment. The goal is to make sure all interested parties understand the breadth of what is involved in establishing a community garden.

Delighted with the response, he starts on the long road of bringing this vision to fruition.

2007-2017

After two solid years of searching for a suitable location, a plot of land in Sabadell, just outside Barcelona, is purchased.

The planning, designing and subsquent construction gets under way.

Finally, after 5 years of working towards the dream, Berardo opens to the public in 2012!

The garden continues to gain traction and by 2017 is self-sufficient! It may have taken a decade, but you reap what you sow!

2017-Now

We continue to grow and community gardens are collaborative by nature.

We are building partnerships between groups, schools, neighbors, businesses and generations - all of which are crucial to the long-term success of Berardo!
